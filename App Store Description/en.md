Your internet, Anywhere — fast, private, and beautifully simple.

Anywhere is the proxy client your iPhone and iPad deserve. No clunky wrappers. No bloated cross-platform shells. Just a clean, native experience built from the ground up in Swift — designed to feel like it belongs on your device, because it does.

Whether you're traveling abroad, working remotely, or just want a more private way to browse, Anywhere connects you in a single tap and gets out of your way.


WHY Anywhere

• One tap to connect. No menus to dig through, no complex dashboards.

• Effortlessly fast. Built natively in Swift, Anywhere launches instantly, sips battery, and routes traffic with the kind of speed only a true native app can deliver.

• Smart routing that just works. Our routing engine automatically picks the fastest path — proxy when you need it, direct when you don't — so pages load faster and videos stream smoother.

• Designed for your country. Headed home for the holidays? Country Bypass keeps your local apps lightning-fast while everything else stays protected.

• Block ads everywhere. Built-in network-level ad blocking cleans up your browser, your apps, and your favorite streaming services. No extra extensions required.

WORKS WITH WHAT YOU HAVE

Anywhere supports the protocols and configurations you already use, including VLESS (with Vision and Reality), Hysteria2, Shadowsocks and more. If your provider gives you a subscription link, Anywhere can handle it.


PRIVACY YOU CAN TRUST

Your traffic flows through your servers. We don't see it, we don't log it, we don't want it. Anywhere is open source under GPLv3, so anyone can verify exactly what we do — and what we don't.
